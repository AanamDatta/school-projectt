Fallingbrook Public School

Fallingbrook P.S. serves a population of 410 students in grades K-8 in Whitby, Ontario. The Fallingbrook school community is a safe and caring one with a focus on academics, safety and character education. The school team works collaboratively to emphasize success for each student.

Located at 155 Fallingbrook St in the neighborhood of Pringle Creek, Fallingbrook Public School is the top rated elementary school in Whitby. With an impressive Fraser Institute score of 8.8 (out of a possible 10) based on evaluations in reading, writing, and math, the school serves kindergarteners through to grade 8.
